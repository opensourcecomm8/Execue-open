About Semantifi
Semantifi is a search software company that helps business and their web customers to search search both structured datasets and unstructured content to deliver 70-80% relevant results vs. 4-5% with keyword search. Semantifi's unique search platform helps businesses to deliver highly relevant knowledge based search results with innovative technologies to:

1) crawl & extract knowledge out of Web pages & datasets,
2) index even multi-terabyte databases to deliver sub-second query results,
3) dynamically aggregate or cluster detailed records into meaningful summaries and,
4) automatically present visuals such as charts and tables for users to easily interpret content.
Semantifi's solutions can drive millions of dollars in reduced costs and increased revenues for leading internet businesses and significantly improved search experience for their end-users/web customers.
