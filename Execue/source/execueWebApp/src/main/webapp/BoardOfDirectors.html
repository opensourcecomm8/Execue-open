Board of Directors
Rajive Johri, Chairman of the Board of Semantifi, Inc.
Rajive currently serves as Director on the boards of Citicorp Trust Bank fsb., ConAgra Foods Inc., Boys Town USA and Lighthouse International. He retired in January 2009 from First National Bank of Omaha where he served as President and Director since 2006. In addition, he served as Director and Executive Vice President for the bank's holding company, First National of Nebraska, Inc., and prior to that served as Director and Chairman for InfiCorp Holdings, Inc. He also served as Director on the boards of Charter Communications Inc. and the Greater Omaha Chamber of Commerce until January 2009.

Rajive brings more than 35 years of US and international executive experience in building businesses and executing business strategies for leading global companies like IBM, Citigroup and J.P. Morgan Chase. Prior to joining First National Bank of Omaha in 2005, Rajive served as Executive Vice President at J.P. Morgan Chase in New York where he was responsible for leading marketing & risk organizations and strategy for their credit card business.

Rajive holds a Bachelor of Technology in Mechanical Engineering from the IIT New Delhi, India and M.B.A in Marketing and Finance from the IIM Calcutta, India.

Shree Pragada, Founder & Chief Executive Officer
Shree oversaw customer marketing across 42 markets as Director of International Cards and lead analytics for an $84B credit card portfolio as Senior Vice President of CitiCards, Citigroup. Shree has over 14 years of experience in marketing, risk management and analytics at Citigroup, Fleet, Chase, and Advanta. Shree has a B. Tech. in Computer Science from Regional Engineering College, Warangal, India and M.S. in Computer and Information Sciences from Temple University, Philadelphia, PA.

Peter V. Longo, President and Executive Director, Connecticut Innovations
Peter manages the venture capital and other technology investment activities of Connecticut Innovations. He has over eleven years of early-stage investing experience in over 20 private equity investments and has served as a director or board representative for nine companies.
Peter is a Certified Public Accountant and a Chartered Financial Analyst. He is a graduate of the University of Connecticut, with a bachelor's degree in business administration, and he holds a master's degree in business administration from the University of Hartford. Peter also served six years in the Connecticut Army National Guard. www.ctinnovations.com
 
Anil Vasagiri, Director of Investments, Connecticut Innovations
Anil joined Connecticut Innovations in 2007, bringing with him more than ten years of experience in business strategy, operations, finance and business development in Fortune 500 high-technology and communications companies including IBM, Oracle, TL Ventures, and Comcast Corporation.

Anil graduated from Regional Engineering College, Durgapur, India, with a bachelor of engineering degree in chemical engineering and earned a master of business administration degree with majors in finance and strategic management from The Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania.
 
Ashoke Dutt, President of Banking and Financial Services, Quinnox, Inc.
Ashoke Dutt heads Quinnox's Banking and Financial Services practice. Ashoke has over 23 years of experience in the global financial services space including corporate and consumer banking. He was formerly Executive Vice President of Marketing at Discover Financial Services, the largest independent credit card network in the US. Ashoke spent three years at Morgan Stanley as Executive Vice President of International before moving on to Discover Financial Services, the credit services business unit of Morgan Stanley.

Prior to his stint at Morgan Stanley, Ashoke spent 20 years at Citibank, where he has held various senior positions in Europe, Asia and the US. Ashoke holds an MS in Industrial Engineering from the Northeastern University, Boston and a BS in Chemical Engineering from Calcutta.
 
Raghav V. Nandagopal, General Partner at Exigen Capital
Prior to Exigen Capital, Raghav was a Senior Vice President of Alternative Investment Group at Bank of America, a Senior Vice President of U.S. Trust Company and Vice President of Excelsior Venture Partners III, LLC. Raghav was the Chief Business Development Officer of Globeshaker, a seed venture capital investment company and an Engagement Manager with McKinsey & Company. In addition, he has over 10 years of experience in numerous technology, management and operations positions at AT&T Capital, BGE, Andersen Consulting.
